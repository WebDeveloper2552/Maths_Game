How to Play

  1. Gameplay: Use ← → arrows to move, ↑ to rotate, ↓ to drop faster, and Space for instant drop.
  2. Math Problems: Solve the math problem shown to clear the current piece and earn points.
  3. Scoring: Earn more points for correct answers, combos, and quick responses.
  4. Level Up: Solve 10 problems to level up and increase game speed.
  5. Game Over: The game ends when blocks reach the top of the board.
